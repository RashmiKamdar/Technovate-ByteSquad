TERMS AND CONDITIONS

✓ By agreeing to donate an organ, the user acknowledges that they are voluntarily choosing to make this decision and are aware of the potential risks and consequences associated with organ donation.


✓The user agrees to undergo a thorough medical evaluation to determine their eligibility as an organ donor. This evaluation may include physical examinations, laboratory tests, and other medical procedures as deemed necessary by medical professionals.


✓The user understands that they have the right to withdraw their consent for organ donation at any time, prior to the actual organ retrieval procedure taking place.


✓The user acknowledges that the decision to donate an organ is irreversible and that they will not be able to retrieve or reclaim the donated organ once it has been transplanted into the recipient


✓The user agrees to provide accurate and truthful information regarding their medical history, lifestyle choices, and any potential risk factors that may affect the suitability of their organ for transplantation.


✓he user understands that the final decision regarding the suitability of their organ for transplantation rests with medical professionals and transplant coordinators, who will consider various factors including medical compatibility, urgency of the recipient's condition, and other ethical considerations.
